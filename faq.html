FAQ

These are our frequently asked questions:


  1. How long will it take for my orders to arrive?
    It will take 5-7 business days and may take longer during holidays or busy periods such as when sales are ongoing

  2. How long will it take for my orders to be prepared/shipped out?
    It usually takes around a week at most. If the status of your order hasn’t changed, notify us via email.

  3. Can I request a refund or cancel my order?
    You may send in a refund request through our email along with the necessary reasoning. Be sure to cancel your order before it is shipped out.

  4. Do you ship internationally?
    Yes with additional rates. Please be sure to check the rates here.

  5. Who I can contact in case of any problems?
    Please refer to our customer service page on the bottom of the page with the chatbox symbol.

  6. Are all of our product legit?
    Yes, they are certified.

  7. A title I want is not available, What are the chances of it being available in your store?
    You may make a request on our form here. If the demand is high enough, we may put it on our shelves.

  8. How often do sales happen?
    Sales are held during peak periods such as when we hold a Mid Year Sale or Christmas Sale.
